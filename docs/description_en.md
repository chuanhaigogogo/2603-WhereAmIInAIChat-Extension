## Detailed Description

Ever been deep in a conversation with AI, learning something important — then suddenly realized you've lost track of what you were originally trying to learn? One question led to another, a side concept popped up, and now you're three topics away from where you started.

MindFlow answers the one question every AI learner keeps asking: **Where am I in this conversation?**


🧠 WHAT IS MINDFLOW?

MindFlow is a Chrome extension that turns any AI conversation into a structured mind map and review notes — so you always know where you are, where you've been, and how every topic connects.

It lives in a resizable sidebar right next to your AI chat. No new tabs, no context switching. Chat on one side, see your map on the other.


🎯 KEY FEATURES

• Mind Map Generation — Your entire AI conversation becomes a visual tree showing every topic, every branch, and every detour you took along the way.

• Structured Review Notes — Alongside the mind map, MindFlow generates notes explaining what each concept is and why it came up in your conversation — perfect for review and revision.

• Flexible Sidebar — Drag the sidebar wider or narrower to fit your workflow. Pinch to zoom into any branch. Resize the font for comfortable reading. Keep it open while you continue chatting.

• Dual View — Open the mind map, the notes, or both side by side. Switch between views anytime.

• Markdown Export — Export or copy your mind map, notes, or both as clean Markdown — ready for Obsidian, Notion, Logseq, or any Markdown-oriented note-taking tool.

• Multi-Platform Support — Works on major AI chat platforms such as ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, and more. See our GitHub page for the full list of supported sites.

• Bring Your Own API Key — Connect your preferred AI provider to power the analysis. Your API key is stored locally in your browser and never sent to our servers.


📖 HOW TO USE

1. Open any supported AI chat platform (such as ChatGPT, Claude, or Gemini).
2. Click the MindFlow icon in your browser toolbar. If the sidebar doesn't appear right away, click the icon again — it may take a moment to load.
3. The sidebar opens next to your conversation.
4. Fill in the API Key you tend to use.
5. Click "Generate" to create a mind map and notes from your conversation.
6. Resize the sidebar, zoom in on branches, or switch between mind map and notes view.
7. When you're done, export as Markdown to save your learning path.

🔒 PRIVACY & DATA

MindFlow respects your privacy. Here's exactly what happens with your data:

- Your API key is stored locally in your browser using Chrome's storage API. It is never transmitted to MindFlow servers.
- Conversation content is read only when you click "Generate." It is sent directly to your chosen AI provider for analysis — MindFlow does not operate any intermediary server.
- No conversation data is collected, stored, or shared by MindFlow.
- You can delete all locally stored data at any time by uninstalling the extension.

Permissions used:

- storage — Save your API key and preferences locally in your browser.
- activeTab — Read conversation content from the current AI chat page when you click the MindFlow icon.

Host permissions are used only to connect directly to your chosen AI provider for mind map generation.

Content scripts run on supported AI chat platforms — only to read conversation text and display the sidebar. See our GitHub page for the full list of supported sites.
